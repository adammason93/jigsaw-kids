Snap

First to 100 cards wins the game. Each round starts with a 5, 4, 3, 2, 1, Go! countdown, then the cards flip for you (auto-flip turns on) until you change it. Flip tiles and look at the two boxes in the middle. When the last two show the same animal (picture and name), the first to tap Snap! on their side takes the whole middle pile and those cards go toward their 100. If the deck runs out, it gets shuffled in again (with any leftover on the table) so you can keep going until someone wins. Tapped too soon (not a real match)? The other player gets a +3 card bonus. Choose characters, which side is yours, then tap Start game.
